WHO WE ARE?

Established in 2024, Drushti is a pioneering NGO committed to transforming lives in underserved communities through holistic initiatives in education, healthcare, and alleviating hunger. With a vision to bring hope and opportunity to those residing in urban slums, Drushti focuses on creating sustainable solutions that uplift communities. Through its educational programs, the organization aims to empower children with knowledge and skills essential for a brighter future.

Drushti also provides vital healthcare services, ensuring access to quality medical care for individuals who would otherwise lack such resources. Additionally, the NGO addresses the pressing issue of hunger by implementing nutrition programs and food distribution initiatives. By addressing these fundamental needs, Drushti is dedicated to fostering positive change and building resilient communities.
